"Specific for set driven key bone animations"

Pre Sets

1 Make sure everything is at their initial position.state
2 Copy model
3 Skin it to skeleton
4 Copy weights from original


Futher steps

1 Detach Skin from original model
2 Move bones to original position (To position where they are offseted to, my case shoulders to 0 in Y axis)
3 Dublicate model
4 Change dublicated model
5 Blend shapes
6 Move bones back to position where they were before
7 smooth bind skin
8 Copy weights from saved model
